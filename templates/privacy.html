Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly to track expenses, we store the data you enter: amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your account information solely to authenticate you and associate your expense records with your account. Your expense data is used only to provide the features of the app — displaying, filtering, and summarising your transactions.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers and protected with standard security practices. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, your personal information and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out via the contact information provided in the app.
